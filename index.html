UpRise SDK - All New

Implimention is lightning fast and earns repeat revenue from your users by using our new UpRise SDK for Android apps.

Our UpRise SDK will ask the user if a new icon can be added to their home screen to help support the development of your app. If permission is granted, we add a new icon that points to our mobile Games and Apps site that has been crafted to generate recurring app revenue and provide real value to the user.

The most lucrative benefit of our UpRise SDK is recurring revenue. Users can access the icon an unlimited amount of times to discover new games and apps. For each new app or offer they try you will earn a 60% revenue share for that referral. Implementation of our UpRise SDK is lightning fast, get started today.

Offer Walls - No SDK

We also offer incentivized and non-incentivized offer walls for Android, iPhone & Windows Phone apps with no SDK required.

Our incentivized offer wall caters to apps with a virtual currency or point system. You can expect high conversion rates and revenue through the incetivized offer wall. Everbadge has a 60% revenue share, with the industry average at only 50% or lower.

Our non-incentivized offer walls act more like an extra feature for your app showing off other great apps. Some devs like to put this on their entrance of the app or the exit. You can also add a "More Apps" or "More Games" button in one of your menus. Have a mobile website? You can use this offer wall here. Revenue share for our non-incetivized offer wall is also 60%.
